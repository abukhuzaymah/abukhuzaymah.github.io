About Me

Welcome! My name is Muhammad Ismath, and I'm an aspiring software engineer based in London. I'm passionate about technology and hope to use my skills to create a meaningful difference in the world while continually challenging myself to grow and learn. I discovered my passion for technology at the DeenDevelopers Hackathon, a community of techies and creatives crowdsourcing talent to build digital solutions for societal problems. Since then, I've attended insight events at places like Twitter and Microsoft, and embarked on a software engineering bootcamp. These experiences have allowed me to connect with other developers and gain exposure to real-world challenges, which has further motivated me to pursue a career in software development. I've worked with a number of partners on various projects, including Microsoft, where my team and I presented an innovative solution for the shipping industry using the Internet of Things (IoT), and developing a solution for travelers with my team alongside the charity Catch22. I always bring my creativity, attention to detail, and commitment to excellence to every project I work on. Thank you for visiting my website, and please feel free to get in touch if you have any questions or if you're interested in working together. I look forward to hearing from you!
